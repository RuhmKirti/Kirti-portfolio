Digital Marketing Conversion Prediction Analysis

Objective: Developed a machine learning model to predict customer conversions in digital marketing campaigns based on demographic and engagement data.

Key Features: Ad Spend, Click Through Rate, Time on Site, Conversion Rate, Loyalty Points, Email Opens.

Methodology: Conducted EDA to identify trends. Preprocessed data using label encoding, scaling, and outlier treatment. Split data into training (80%) and testing (20%) sets.

Models Used: Logistic Regression (87% Accuracy), Decision Tree, Random Forest, SVM (Strong F1-score).

Outcome: Enabled improved targeting and resource allocation for marketing campaigns, reducing inefficiencies and enhancing ROAS.
